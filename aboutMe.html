More About Me

Where my programming journey started:

When I was in high school, I joined the schools robotics team on a wimb. That decision led me to where I am today and helped me chose computer science as my career path. I started as a grade nine student who knew nothing about programming or robotics and ended as the programming leader and one of the core parts of the team. That journey taught me many things aside from programming such as perseverance, teamwork and leadership. I also gained knowledge in C++ and Java programming. As a graduate, I now go back to my high school and other teams to mentor and help kids like me to fall in love with programming.

My life outside programming:

I love both being out with friends and also being at home all alone snuggled in my bed with a good show on the TV.
